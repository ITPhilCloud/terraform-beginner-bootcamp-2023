Tuna Burgers

A very easy to make and healthy meal, prepared in just a few minutes.

Ingredients:

  • one egg
  • one 195g tin of tuna in sunflower oil
  • 35g fine oatflakes
  • half of a teaspoon oregano
  • optional: a quarter finely chopped onion
  • optional: a tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl until sticky mass. Divide into equal parts.
  2. Heat good cooking oil in a pan.
  3. Shape burgers and fry until golden brown each side.

Protein shake

This energy shake is big on protein.

Ingredients:

  • 250g raspberries, blueberries, strawberries, or pineapple pieces
  • half of a banana
  • 500ml non sweet and cooled Soja Drink
  • a tablespoon fine oat flakes
  • 40g peeled hemp or flax seed
  • a tablespoon milled almonds (alternatively almond butter)
  • optional: a tablespoon finely chopped coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Blend well and serve in glasses. Best served chilled.
